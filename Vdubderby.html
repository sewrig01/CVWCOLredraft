V-DUB DERBY

The 2015 V-Dub Derby was a great success! Thank you to everyone who attended and made the day. We had a record 65 cars registered at the show. We keep growing every year and that is attributed to our wonderful sponsors and great volunteers from the day. Thanks for everyone making the day a success!

Mark your calenders for next year! The V-Dub Derby 2016 will be: Saturday, September 10, 2016 9am-2pm
